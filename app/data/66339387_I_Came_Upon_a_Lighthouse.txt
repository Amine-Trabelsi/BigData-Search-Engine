I Came Upon a Lighthouse: A Short Memoir of Life with Ratan Tata is a memoir written by Shantanu Naidu and illustrated by Sanjana Desai. Published by HarperCollins India in January 2021, the memoir explores the life and relation between the millennial Naidu and the octogenarian industrialist, Ratan Tata. \- The book is an illustrated memoir of Naidu's candid interaction with Tata over five years. \- == References == Category:Indian literature in English Category:Debut books Category:2021 non-fiction books Category:Indian memoirs
