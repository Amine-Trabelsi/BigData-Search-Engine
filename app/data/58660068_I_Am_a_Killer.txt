I Am a Killer is a series on Netflix and Crime+Investigation UK featuring interviews with death row inmates. Season 2 aired in the United Kingdom in 2019 and aired internationally on Netflix from January 31, 2020. On August 30, 2022, Season 3 aired on Netflix in the United States.
