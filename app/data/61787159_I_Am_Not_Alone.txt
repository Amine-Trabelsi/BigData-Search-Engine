I Am Not Alone is an Armenian-American documentary film, directed by Garin Hovannisian and released in 2019.Brian Welk, "‘I Am Not Alone’ Trailer: Armenia’s ‘Velvet Revolution’ Starts With a Step in New Documentary". TheWrap, September 6, 2019. The film profiles Armenian politician Nikol Pashinyan and his role in the 2018 Armenian revolution.Graham Isador, "System of a Down's Serj Tankian on Making Music for a Revolution". Vice, September 13, 2019. Music for the film was composed by Serj Tankian, who also participated as an executive producer. The film premiered at the 2019 Toronto International Film Festival, where it was named first runner-up for the People's Choice Award for Documentaries.Jessica Wong, "TIFF 2019: Jojo Rabbit captures TIFF People's Choice Award".
