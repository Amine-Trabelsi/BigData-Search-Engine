"I Am Beautiful" is the debut single and coronation song from American recording artist and American Idol season 12 winner, Candice Glover. The song was co-written by Joleen Belle and Jaden Michaels, and co-produced by Roahn Hylton and John Lock. Her recording of "I Am Beautiful" was released as a single on May 16, 2013. An acoustic version of the song appeared on her debut album, Music Speaks as a bonus track. The original is not included on the album. ==Live performances== Glover first performed the song on the season's final performance night on May 15, 2013, and then again on the finale after she was declared the winner. Glover then performed the single on The Tonight Show with Jay Leno the following night. On May 20, 2013, Glover performed on Live! with Kelly and Michael. ==Track listing== ==Chart performance== In its first week of release, the single sold 48,000 copies in the US. The song debuted at No. 5 on the R&B; Digital Songs chart, No. 6 on the Heatseekers Songs chart, No. 33 on the Hot R&B;/Hip-Hop Songs chart and No. 93 on the Hot 100.
