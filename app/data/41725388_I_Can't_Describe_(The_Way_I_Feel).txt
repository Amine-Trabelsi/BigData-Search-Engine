"I Can't Describe (The Way I Feel)" is a song by American recording artist Jennifer Hudson, released worldwide on September 24, 2013 as the lead single from her third studio album, JHUD (2014). The song, (with virtually zero elemental changes) was originally intended to be recorded by American artist Miley Cyrus, during sessions for her album, Bangerz, with the rap verse performed by American rapper Tyler, The Creator. However, the track was not finalized, and approved on the album. The official release was instead released by Hudson, with the rap verse performed by American rapper T.I.
