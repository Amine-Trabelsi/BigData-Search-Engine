"I Can't Hear the Music" is a song by American R&B; group Brutha, released September 30, 2008 by The Island Def Jam Music Group, as the lead single from their self-titled debut album, Brutha (2008). The song, which also serves as their debut single, was produced by Blac Elvis and features a guest verse from American rapper Fabolous. ==Music video== The music video for "I Can't Hear the Music" was directed by Jessy Terrero, produced by Josh Goldstein.
