I Am Sam is the soundtrack to the 2001 film I Am Sam."New Soundtrack = All- star Beatles Covers". Chart Attack, November 7, 2001 It was released on January 8, 2002 by V2 Records (see 2002 in music). The album contents are made up entirely of cover versions of songs by The Beatles, although it was originally intended to consist of the group's original recordings.
