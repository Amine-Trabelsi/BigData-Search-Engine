I Am is a series of documentary films about various prominent celebrity and political figures produced by Canadian company Network Entertainment, most of which were originally commissioned by United States cable network Paramount Network (formerly Spike TV). The series has regularly aired on Crave in Canada, and was picked up for rebroadcast by The CW in the U.S. beginning in fall 2023.
