I Could Never Have Sex with Any Man Who Has So Little Regard for My Husband is a 1973 American sex comedy film directed by Robert McCarty, from a screenplay by Dan Greenburg, based on his 1968 novel Chewsday. The film stars Carmine Caridi, Andrew Duncan, Cynthia Harris, and Lynne Lipton. ==Synopsis== Two middle-aged pairs of aspiring swingers rent a summer cottage in Martha's Vineyard, Massachusetts.
