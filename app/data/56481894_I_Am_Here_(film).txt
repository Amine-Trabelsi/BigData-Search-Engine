I Am Here is a Canadian short animated film, directed by Eoin Duffy and released in 2016."Q&A;: Eoin Duffy on his new animated short film". Vancouver Magazine, September 30, 2016. The film relates the tale of an intrepid explorer (voiced by Nicholas Campbell) who has travelled the universe in search of ultimate meaning, only to discover that what he was looking for never really existed."CB Fest Premiere: ‘I Am Here’ by Eoin Duffy". Cartoon Brew, November 5, 2017.
