I Am an Island is the debut studio album from Scottish alternative rock band Fatherson and was released April 7, 2014. The album includes the singles "I Like Not Knowing" and "Mine for Me". == Singles == * "I Like Not Knowing" was released as the album's lead single on March 26, 2014. * "Mine for Me" was released as the album's second single on July 9, 2014. == Track listing == == Release history == Country Release date Format Label United Kingdom 7 April 2014 Digital download A Modern Way 7 April 2014 CD ==References== Category:2014 debut albums Category:Fatherson albums
