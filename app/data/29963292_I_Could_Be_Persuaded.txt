"I Could Be Persuaded" is a song co-written and recorded by American country music duo The Bellamy Brothers. It was released in June 1990 as the first single from the album Reality Check. The song reached number 7 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart. It was the duo's last Top 10 hit.
