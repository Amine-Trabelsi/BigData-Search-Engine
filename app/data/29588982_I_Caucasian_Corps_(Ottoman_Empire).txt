The I Caucasian Corps of the Ottoman Empire (Turkish: 1 nci Kafkas Kolordusu or Birinci Kafkas Kolordusu) was one of the corps of the Ottoman Army. It was formed during World War I. ==Formations== === Order of Battle, December 1916, August 1917, January 1918 === In December 1916, August 1917, January 1918, the corps was structured as follows:Edward J.
