Ana Horra or Ana Hurra (, ) is a 1959 Egyptian drama starring Lobna Abdel Aziz. Released in 1959, the film captures much of the feminist sentiments of the 1960s and its widespread fervor in Egypt. The film's protagonist, Amina, played by Lubna Abdel Aziz, is a young woman living with her aunt's family which includes her uncle and her cousin.
