"I Believe" is a song that was performed by the Canadian group Chilliwack. It was released on their 1981 album Wanna Be a Star. In Canada, the song peaked at number 13 for two weeks. In the United States, it reached number 33 on the Billboard Hot 100Joel Whitburn's Top Pop Singles 1955-1990 - and number 29 on Cash Box.
