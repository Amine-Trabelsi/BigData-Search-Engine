"I Almost Lost My Mind" is a popular song written by Ivory Joe Hunter and published in 1950. Hunter's recording of the song was a number one hit on the US Billboard R&B; singles chart in that year. Hunter recorded the 12-bar blues style song on October 1, 1949, and became a rhythm and blues hit and a pop standard. The best selling version of the song was a cover version by Pat Boone, which reached number one on the Billboard charts in 1956.
