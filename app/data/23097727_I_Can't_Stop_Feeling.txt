"I Can't Stop Feeling" is a song by German girl group Queensberry. It was written by Teemu Brunila and Kid Crazy and produced by John McLaughlin and Dave James for their debut album Volume I (2008). Serving as their debut, it was released as a double A-side, backed with their 2008 promotional debut single "No Smoke", on 20 February 2009.
