"I Am Woman" is a song by American singer Emmy Meli, released on November 18, 2021, through Arista and Disruptor Records. The song went viral on video sharing app TikTok. ==Background== Emmy Meli is a singer from California who gained popularity after posting the song to TikTok, where it has over 22 million views.
