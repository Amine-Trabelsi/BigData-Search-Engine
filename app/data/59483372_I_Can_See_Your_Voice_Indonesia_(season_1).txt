The Indonesian television mystery music game show I Can See Your Voice Indonesia premiered the inaugural first season on MNCTV on 29 August 2016. ==Gameplay== ===Format=== Under the original format, the guest artist can eliminate one or two mystery singers after each round. The game concludes with the last mystery singer standing which depends on the outcome of a duet performance with a guest artist. ====Rewards==== If the singer is good, he/she will feature on a privilege video; if the singer is bad, he/she wins . ===Rounds=== Each episode presents the guest artist with seven people whose identities and singing voices are kept concealed until they are eliminated to perform on the "stage of truth" or remain in the end to perform the final duet. ==Episodes== === Guest artists === Legend: Episode Guest artist Mystery singers Elimination order Winner 1 29 August 2016 width="10%" width="10%" colspan="2" width="10%" width="10%" width="10%" width="10%" 2 5 September 2016 Vina Panduwinata colspan="2" 3 12 September 2016 Delon Thamrin colspan="2" 4 19 September 2016 colspan="2" 5 26 September 2016 Rizky Febian colspan="2" 6 3 October 2016 colspan="2" 7 10 October 2016 colspan="2" 8 17 October 2016 colspan="2" 9 24 October 2016 colspan="2" 10 31 October 2016 Cita Citata colspan="2" 11 7 November 2016 Fatin Shidqia colspan="2" 12 14 November 2016 colspan="2" 13 21 November 2016 Melly Goeslaw width="10%" width="10%" === Panelists === Legend: Episode Panelists 1 rowspan="13" rowspan="13" rowspan="3" rowspan="5" rowspan="2" rowspan="2" 2 3 4 rowspan="5" colspan="2" Vacant 5 rowspan="4" 6 7 8 rowspan="6" 9 rowspan="2" 10 rowspan="2" 11 12 13 ==Notes== ==References== Category:I Can See Your Voice Indonesia Category:2016 Indonesian television seasons
