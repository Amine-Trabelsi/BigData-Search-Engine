I Am Comic is a 2010 documentary about the stand-up comedy world directed by Jordan Brady. ==Overview== Directed by former stand-up comic Jordan Brady and starring and narrated by ex-comedian turned comedy writer Ritch Shydner, the film explores the world of stand-up comedy and features interviews with some of the top comedians working today. The film had its world premiere at the 2010 Slamdance Film Festival and is distributed by Monterey Media and IFC Films. ==Festivals== I Am Comic has been screened at the following film festivals: * The Feel Good Film Festival * Laugh Your Ashville Off Comedy Festival * Just For Laughs Comedy Festival * Cinefamily's Comedy Festival * Little Rock Film Festival * Slamdance * Bridgetown Comedy Festival * Atlanta Film Festival * Kansas City Jubilee * James River Film Festival * Laugh Factory V.I.P.
