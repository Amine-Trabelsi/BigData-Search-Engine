"I Can't Get Enough" is a song by Swedish singer Cazzi Opeia, released as a single on 19 February 2022. It was performed in Melodifestivalen 2022 and qualified to the final on 12 March 2022. ==Melodifestivalen 2022== "I Can't Get Enough" was performed in Heat 3 on 19 February 2022. She garnered 63 points and qualified to the semi-final.
