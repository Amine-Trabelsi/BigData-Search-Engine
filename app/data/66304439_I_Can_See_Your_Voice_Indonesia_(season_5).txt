The Indonesian television mystery music game show I Can See Your Voice Indonesia was initially scheduled to premiere the fifth season on MNCTV on 18 January 2021, but it was delayed until it happened on 25 January 2021. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, this programme is filmed under health and safety protocols being implemented for this season. ==Gameplay== ===Format=== Under the original format, the guest artist can eliminate one or two mystery singers after each round. The game concludes with the last mystery singer standing which depends on the outcome of a duet performance with a guest artist. ====Rewards==== If the singer is good, he/she will feature on a privilege video; if the singer is bad, he/she wins . ===Rounds=== Each episode presents the guest artist with six people whose identities and singing voices are kept concealed until they are eliminated to perform on the "stage of truth" or remain in the end to perform the final duet. ==Episodes== === Guest artists === Legend: Episode Guest artist Mystery singers Elimination order Winner 1 25 January 2021 Dewi Persik width="12.5%" width="12.5%" width="12.5%" width="12.5%" width="12.5%" width="12.5%" 2 1 February 2021 3 8 February 2021 4 15 February 2021 Titi DJ 5 22 February 2021 Inul Daratista 6 1 March 2021 Maia Estianty 7 8 March 2021 Ikke Nurjanah 8 15 March 2021 9 22 March 2021 Onsu Dynasty 10 29 March 2021 Via Vallen 11 5 April 2021 12 12 April 2021 and === Panelists === Legend: Episode Panelists 1 rowspan="12" rowspan="6" rowspan="2" rowspan="2" rowspan="5" Vacant 2 rowspan="2" rowspan="2" 3 rowspan="6" rowspan="2" 4 rowspan="9" 5 rowspan="2" 6 rowspan="3" 7 rowspan="2" rowspan="3" Vacant 8 rowspan="5" 9 10 rowspan="3" 11 rowspan="2" Vacant 12 ==Notes== ==References== Category:I Can See Your Voice Indonesia Category:2021 Indonesian television seasons Category:Television series impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic
