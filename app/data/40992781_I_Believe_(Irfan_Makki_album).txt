I Believe is the second studio album by the Canadian Muslim singer and songwriter Irfan Makki on Awakening Records. The album was released on July 28, 2011. ==Track listing== The original version of the album contains 13 tracks. Irfan Makki – I Believe , Islamic Books, Islamic Audio, Video, Software and more!.
