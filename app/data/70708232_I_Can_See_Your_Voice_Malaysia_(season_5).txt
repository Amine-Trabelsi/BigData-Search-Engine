The Malaysian Malay-language television mystery music game show I Can See Your Voice Malaysia premiered the fifth season on TV3 on 15 May 2022, following an Eid al-Fitr special that aired on 8 May 2022. ==Gameplay== ===Format=== Under the original format, the guest artist can eliminate one or two mystery singers after each round. The game concludes with the last mystery singer standing which depends on the outcome of a duet performance with a guest artist. ====Rewards==== If the singer is good, he/she will have chance to get a potential contract and a single on any record label; if the singer is bad, he/she wins . ===Rounds=== Each episode presents the guest artist with six people whose identities and singing voices are kept concealed until they are eliminated to perform on the "stage of truth" or remain in the end to perform the final duet. ==Episodes== ===Guest artists=== Legend: Episode Guest artist Mystery singers Elimination order Winner colspan="9" Special 8 May 2022 and width="12.5%" width="12.5%" width="12.5%" width="12.5%" width="12.5%" width="12.5%" colspan="9" 1 15 May 2022 and MK () 2 22 May 2022 3 29 May 2022 Dolla 4 5 June 2022 and Ella 5 12 June 2022 Zamani Ibrahim 6 19 June 2022 and 7 26 June 2022 and Shila Amzah 8 3 July 2022 9 10 July 2022 and 10 17 July 2022 Janna Nick 11 24 July 2022 Siti Nurhaliza === Panelists === Legend: Episode Panelists Special rowspan=12 rowspan=10 rowspan=6 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 rowspan=5 8 9 10 Vacant 11 ==Online Concert== For this season, all of winning good singers perform through online concert, in which one of them would grant an exclusive contract and a single on any record label.
